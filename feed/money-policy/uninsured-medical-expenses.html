How to check non-covered medical expenses before visiting the hospital ​

non-covered medical expenses ​

non-covered medical expenses

  • You can search for non-covered medical expenses in advance through the internet and mobile before visiting the hospital.
  • Non-covered items refer to those not covered by medical insurance, and the cost of non-covered medical expenses varies greatly between hospitals, which often causes confusion and burden for patients.
  • Starting from September 5, you can search for non-covered medical expenses by hospital through the Health Insurance Review and Assessment Service (HIRA) website and the mobile application Health e-um.
  • This year, the prices of 623 non-covered items, including MRI, ultrasound tests, and vaccinations, will be disclosed first for all medical institutions.
  • According to the government’s 2024 survey of non-covered medical expenses, the average price for 65.7% of the total items has increased. For example, the average price of manual therapy, a high-interest item for the public, increased by 2.5% compared to last year, and shingles vaccination costs rose by 10.6%. In particular, prices for manual therapy and multifocal lenses for cataract surgery varied significantly by hospital. For example, in Seoul, Clinic A charges 100,000 won for manual therapy, while Clinic B in South Gyeongsang charges 260,000 won. As for multifocal lenses, Clinic C in Seoul charges around 290,000 won, while Clinic D charges 6.8 million won, showing a price difference of more than 20 times.

How to Search ​

How to Search

  • On the Health Insurance Review and Assessment Service (HIRA) website, click on the menu Inquiries & Applications → Non-Covered Medical Expenses Information.
  • On the Detailed Search for Non-Covered Medical Expenses screen, enter the region, type of medical institution, and non-covered medical expense item, then confirm.
  • The median price of institutions of similar size in the region will be displayed, so there’s no need to click on every hospital individually.
